GPC Reference by Stage

Stage 1 (K-Fall): Core Consonants & Short Vowels

m, s, a, t, n, p, i, d, f, o, l, h, b, e, u — 15 letters, short vowels only, CVC words

Stage 2 (K-Spring): Remaining Letters & Digraphs

r, g, c, k, w, y, v, z, j, x, qu, th, sh, ch, wh, ng, ck

Stage 3 (1st-Fall): VCe Long Vowels & FLOSS

a_e, i_e, o_e, u_e, e_e, ff, ll, ss, zz + open syllables (he, she, we, be, me, no, so, go)

Stage 4 (1st-Spring): Vowel Teams

ai, ay, ee, ea→/ē/, oa, ow→/ō/, igh, y→/ī/, y→/ē/, o→/ŭ/

Stage 5 (2nd-Fall): R-Controlled & /oo/

ar, er, ir, ur, or, ore, oo, oul, wa, wor, war, our

Stage 6 (2nd-Spring): Diphthongs

ou→/ow/, ow→/ow/, oi, oy, au, aw, eigh, ey→/ā/, ere

Stage 7 (3rd-Fall): Complex Patterns

soft c, soft g, ough, ch→/k/, ch→/sh/, ve, se→/z/

Stage 8 (3rd-Spring): Morphology & Schwa

un-, re-, pre-, dis-, -ed, -s/-es, -ing, -tion, -sion, schwa
